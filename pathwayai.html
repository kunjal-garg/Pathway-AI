Why PathwayAI exists.

We built PathwayAI because career guidance for students is broken. Most advice is generic, resources are outdated, and the “right path” depends on your role, your background, and the current job market.

PathwayAI fixes this by using live job market signals and AI to translate what companies want into a clear, actionable plan you can follow week by week.

Student-built from day one — so every screen, every question, and every output is designed to reduce anxiety and increase clarity.

How to get your LinkedIn data:

  1. Go to linkedin.com → Click your profile picture (top right)
  2. Click "Settings & Privacy"
  3. In the left sidebar click "Data privacy"
  4. Under "How LinkedIn uses your data" click "Download your data"
  5. Select "Want something in particular? Select the data files you're most interested in."
  6. Check this box: ✅ Profile — Profile contains your education, positions, skills, and summary
  7. Click "Request archive"
  8. LinkedIn will say "Your download will be ready in about 10 mins" — it's usually instant. Check your email for the download link.
  9. Download the ZIP file and upload it below ↓
